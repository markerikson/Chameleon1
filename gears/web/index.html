As a Senior Software Engineering project, the Digital Gearheads will be designing and devloping CHAMELEON. Chameleon is an adaptive Integrated Developement Environment designed for instructional purposes. The goal of the Chameleon I.D.E. is to provide an environment with which an introductory computer science students can be comfortable in, and also have the ability to adapt with the student as the skill and functionality needs of the student change.
