As a Senior Software Engineering project, the Digital Gearheads will be designing and developing Chameleon. Chameleon is an adaptive Integrated Developement Environment designed for instructional purposes. The goal of Chameleon is to provide an environment which is suitable for introductory computer science students, and to also have the ability to adapt with the student as the skill and functionality needs of the student change.
