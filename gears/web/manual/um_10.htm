1.0 Introduction

Welcome to Chameleon, the Adaptive Integrated Development Environment.

1.1 What is Chameleon?

Chameleon is an Itegrate Development Environment (IDE for short). It is a single program that has consolidated programming tools and functions into one unified environment to assist in code development.

1.2 When or Why Do I Use it?

Chameleon is designed for, and most appropriate for beginning C/C++ programmers in an instructional environment.

1.3 Who wrote it?

Chameleon was designed and written by Mark Erikson, Ben Carhart, and David Czechowski as their senior design project during the 2003-2004 school year at Cedarville University.
