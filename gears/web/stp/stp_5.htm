5.0 Test Log

Simply state that this section provides a log outline for recording the results of the test cases. The tester is expected to print a hard copy of this section, so that the physical paper may be used to record the results. However, after the tests are complete, this section shall be updated (on-line) by the tester to provide an electronic record of the test log.
