2.0 Project Organization

2.1 Process Model

Our team plans to use the incremental model with many Feature Driven Development principles. At least initially, we intend to use the general 5-phase process of FDD:

  1. Develop an Overall Model
  2. Build a Features List
  3. Plan by Feature
  4. Design by Feature
  5. Build by Feature

2.3 Organizational Boundaries and Interfaces

While each team member has been given with certain roles, day-to-day interaction will be relatively informal and may vary depending on the tasks being performed.  For instance, the whole team has sketched out an initial design concept, with each team member responsible for fleshing out individual sections.  Team members have primary responsibility for their sections, but the others might work on them as necessary later in the project..
