7.0 Design Analysis

7.1 Alternatives

The design of each module is a matter of decision for the designer / coder. As such there have been various suggested models suggested for interactions among elements. One such design had the GUI as the central communicating entity in the product. As it is, our team has agreed on specific interfaces as listed in section 5.X of this document.

7.2 Rationale

The only mentioned alternative was rejected as inappropriate for the amount of communication desired between modules.
