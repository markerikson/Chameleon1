7.0 Design Analysis

7.1 Alternatives

As stated in the PMP, our team is taking a class ownership approach. As a result, the design details modules is a matter of decision for the designer / coder. The overall design model, was decided on without much disagreement because of how naturally the partitioning occurs. One similar design that was considered had a similar partitioning, but had the GUI as the central communicating entity in the product. Instead our team has agreed on specific interfaces as listed in section 5.X of this document.

7.2 Rationale

The aforementioned alternative was rejected as inefficient due to the amount of communication desired between modules, and the additional complexity that was unnecessary.
