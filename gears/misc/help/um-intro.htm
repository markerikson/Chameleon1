Introduction

Welcome to Chameleon, the Adaptive Integrated Development Environment.

What is Chameleon?

Chameleon is an Integrated Development Environment (IDE for short). It is a single program that combines programming tools and functions into one unified environment to assist in code development.

When or Why Do I Use it?

Chameleon is designed for, and most appropriate for beginning C/C++ programmers in an instructional environment.

Who wrote it?

Chameleon was designed and written by Mark Erikson, Ben Carhart, and David Czechowski as their senior design project during the 2003-2004 school year at Cedarville University. Mark resumed active development on version 1.1+ in October 2006.
